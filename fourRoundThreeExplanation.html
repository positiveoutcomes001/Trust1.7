Round Three

In this round both remaining players will be given the choice to remain loyal to their alliance or to betray it.

If both players remain loyal to their alliance, then they shall both be declared joint winners and shall split the game prize.

If one player chooses to betray the alliance while the other chooses to remain loyal then the betrayer wins and shall keep all of the game prize.

If both players betray the alliance, then both shall be permanently eliminated and will lose their secondary contributions which will go into the game prize.

The two previously eliminated players shall be re-enter the game to play for the game prize.

Again, players are free to discuss their intentions but are not allowed to see the each make their choices!
