Trust

Round One

In this round each player will choose which other player they wish to form an alliance with.

If each player is chosen by another then no alliance will be formed, and each player's contribution to the game prize will be returned.

If two players choose to form an alliance, then the other player shall be eliminated. However, this does not mean that the eliminated player can't still win the game prize!

Players can discuss their decisions before making them. You can tell the truth about your intentions or lie. Whichever you think is best!

However, players must make their decisions OUT OF SIGHT of the other players!
